About Me
Passionate iOS Developer with a Diverse Background:

I'm a highly motivated iOS programmer with a strong foundation in various technical areas. My journey in IT took an unexpected turn, but each experience has fueled my passion for building user-centric mobile applications.

My initial foray began with Telecommunications Engineering, where I devoured every programming course available. Recognizing my deep interest, I switched to Computer Engineering to delve deeper. It was during this time that I dabbled in mobile app development, quickly realizing the vast technical knowledge required. Determined to master this field, I enrolled in a life-changing mobile application programming bootcamp with KeepCoding. This experience solidified my passion for iOS development.


From Bootcamp Graduate to Mobile Developer:

After completing the bootcamp, I was offered a position at Equilibrha, where I tackled diverse challenges:

  • Maintaining legacy code in Java (Android) and Objective-C (iOS) for existing applications.
  • Building two new employee time management applications from scratch, leveraging Jetpack Compose for Android and SwiftUI for iOS.
  • Developing functionalities for integrating META4 into HR management software.

Continuous Learning and Sharing Knowledge:
Currently, I'm actively involved in two personal projects:
  • An app to organize shopping products.
  • An app exploring the token economy, developed collaboratively with a group of 12 programmers.

Beyond development, I'm passionate about sharing my knowledge. I teach iOS programming modules at KeepCoding, covering topics like UIKit, SwiftUI, Secure Development, and Design Patterns with VIPER.
